Streams Manager

This is where you manage your favorite web-radio stations, where you access to a preset of web-radio and where you can import your iTunes library web-radio.

  • Your favorites web-radio is on the "Favorites" tab. The list shows the names and the URLs of your streams:
    • Double-click on a cell to change its contents.
    • To remove a stream, select the line and click the “-” button.
    • To add a stream or a folder to the list, click the “+” button.
      A contextual menu pop-up, and let you add an empty favorite, a favorite filled with the actual playing informations or a folder.
    Once your favorites web-radio added in the list, you can access them via the upper half-circle in the main window, or via the status menu.
  • The list of web-radios presets is on the “Preset” tab. You have access to the presets of ShoutCast and SourceMac.
  • The list of web-radios of your iTunes library is on the “iTunes” tab.
 To play a web-radio, double-click on the web-radio name, and to stop it click on the button “Stop”. To add a web-radio on your playlist, select it and click on “Add”.
