Quick Open

FStream allows you to play a web-radio "on-the-go".

  • Play a web-radio without add it on your favorites:
    QuickOpen allows you to quickly load a single stream. Just enter the URL and click the “Open” button. FStream load the radio stream and immediately begins playing.
