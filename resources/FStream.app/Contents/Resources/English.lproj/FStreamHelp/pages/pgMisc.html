Miscellaneous

FStream have some little extra features. You can find here most of them.

  • The Apple IR Remote Control:
    The remote controller furnished with the new Macs is fully supported.
    • The Play/Pause button of the controller has the same behavior as the Play/Pause button in the FStream interface.
    • The Previous/Next button allows changing of radio stations per your list order.
    • The volume buttons change FStream’s volume.
    • Holding the Play/Pause button for a few seconds will start or stop a recording session.
  • Opening Playlist Files (.pls and .m3u) in FStream:
    You can open .pls or .m3u files in FStream directly from the Finder in any of several different ways…
    • Drag-and-drop the file on FStream’s icon, in FStream’s main window, or in the Streams Manager window list.
    • Control-click (or right-click) on the file’s icon, hover over “Open With” in the contextual menu, and choose “FStream” from the fly-out menu.
    • To define FStream as the default application for opening this type of file:
      • choose one of the files in the Finder and open its information dialog (Ctrl-click [right-click] and choose “Get Info”; type Cmd-I; in column view, click the “More info...” button; or click on the Finder’s File menu and choose “Get Info”);
      • in the Info dialog, click on the disclosure triangle next to the “Open with:” pane if it isn't already open, click on the pop-up menu and choose FStream, then click on the “Change All...” button.
