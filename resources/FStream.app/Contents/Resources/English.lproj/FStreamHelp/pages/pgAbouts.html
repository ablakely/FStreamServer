Information & Credits

FStream use a lot of external modules. Thank to them for there work.

  • Icon by Nicolas Péré (www.amarok2.net)
  • Audio Engine based on FSSound (SourceMac private class)
  • Network Engine based on the NetSocket class (www.blackholemedia.com)
  • MP3 Encoding Engine based on LAME (lame.sourceforge.net)
  • MP3 Decoding Engine based on LibMad (www.underbit.com)
  • OGG Vorbis Decoding Engine based on LibVorbisFile & LibVorbis (www.vorbis.com) and on LibOGG (www.xiph.org)
  • AAC Decoding Engine based on LibFAAD (www.audiocoding.com)
  • Re-sampling Engine based on the Secret Rabbit Code (aka libsamplerate) (www.mega-nerd.com/SRC/)
  • Grandient Engine based on CTGradient (blog.oofn.net)
  • AppleRemote based on Remote Control Wrapper (www.martinkahr.com)
  • Equalizer inspired from VLC [code by Laurent Aimar] (www.videolan.org)
  • ASF parsing based on libasf (code.google.com/p/libasf/)
  • MMS protocol based on libmms (launchpad.net/libmms)
  • WMA Decoding Engine inspired from ffmpeg (ffmpeg.mplayerhq.hu)
  • FStream build by Avérous Julien-Pierre in 2005-2008 (www.sourcemac.com , j.averous@sourcemac.com)
